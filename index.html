Moving To The States

It is well known that the USA, by far, has the largerst number of Immigrants. Have you yourself ever thought about relocating to the states? This report will take you through a journey of discovering the best field of employment and location of state based on your own preferences.

Get A Job

The question that most people have before commiting to migrating to a different country is "Will l find a job?". The plot shown aside visualises the top 60 jobs which had the most advertised job vacancies in 2022.

It can be seen that Nurses had the most advertised job vacancies by a large gap, closely followed by Delivery Drivers and Sales Support Assistant .

In contrast the jobs with the highest (average) advertised salaries are both engineering related with Nursing jobs coming in the second.

The question then becomes whats more important getting a job without too much hustle or getting paid a lot more than the average american earner?









Choose Your State

A desirable location in this context may be the one that has a low unemployment rate which makies it be easier for one to find a job. Others however may prefer making a decision based on the geographical location.

Florida, Carlifornia and Texas have an unemployment rate of 0.01, making them favourable for the average job hunter.

The State Alaska, on the flip side, has the highest rate of Unemployment, followed by the state of Wyoming.











Hot Or Cold

Some people prefer Hot weather whilst others prefer cold. The Map Aside shows the average seasonal temperature of each state.

If One prefers Hot weather, coast line states like Florida, Louisiana, and Texas will be the best options. If One however prefers Cold weather, upper north states like North Dekota, Minnesota and Wisconsin would be suitable.

The Landlocked states like Kansas typically have moderate temperatures compared to the outer states through out the seasons.



















Cost Of Living

In most cases, it all comes down to the cost of living. How much does it actually cost to live in a state. The USA is counted to be one of the most expensive places to reside in on a global scale.

States like Hawaii, Carlifornia, New York, Massachusetts, Alaska, Vermont New Hampshire and The District Of Colombia are observed to be expensive in all five expense categories

On the other side, states like Missouri, Oklahoma, Arkansas, Louisiana and Mississippi are observed to be fair valued in all five expense categories.

As one would expect, the cost housing is observed to be have the highest expense rate. The visual goes on to show that health and grocery expenses have the second highest expense rate, which is expected for the later but not so much for the former
